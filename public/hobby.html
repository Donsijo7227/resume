Hobbies

  • Coding personal projects - this demonstrates your passion for coding and shows that you
    are dedicated to improving your skills outside of work.
  • Attending tech conferences and meetups - this shows that you are interested in staying
    up-to-date with the latest technologies and networking with other professionals in your field.
  • Contributing to open-source projects - this demonstrates your commitment to the open-source
    community and your ability to work collaboratively on projects.
  • Blogging about tech topics - this shows that you are knowledgeable about your field and
    that you are capable of communicating complex ideas in a clear and concise manner.
  • Playing online games - this may not seem relevant, but it can demonstrate your ability to
    problem-solve, work collaboratively, and think strategically.
